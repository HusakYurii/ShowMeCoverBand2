ShowMe is an exciting cover band from Kiev (Kyiv) who will transform your casual celebration into an awesome party. If you love the best and the latest in popular music - we are exactly what you need! Cover band Showme will immerse the captive audience with drive and positive emotions. The band consists of five professional musicians (female vocal, keyboard instrument, guitar, bass guitar, and drums).

The musicians of the cover band thoroughly learn the songs accompaniment, preserving the stylistic features of the song and the cover band Showme always performs live music without using recorded playbacks or phonograms.You can check this out for yourself by watching the promotional video. Musicians play in such styles as pop, funk, light jazz, and lounge.

The repertoire of the band is updated and refreshed almost every day. Showme always tries to meet the needs and expectations of the customer and all requests and suggestions are discussed in advance. By playing the most popular music, the musicians make the emphasis on the uniqueness of each and every event.

Guitar
Yaroslav Babanin

Ideological inspirational figure and creator of the band. Before creating the band Showme, he was performing with several Kyiv bands. He possesses good improvisation skills and deals with the matters concerning the audio recording.

Vocal Alina Vynnyk

Alina sings from the age of five and she is a winner of many international and all-Ukrainian music competitions. Alina has finished musical school and university of culture. She was the singer in cover band UnDoStrees.

Drums Oleksandr Kelbas

Ex-drummer of NRavitsa Planet band, participated in talent show “X-Factor”, “Mamahohotala” TV-show, “Atlas Weekend” festival, worked together with Nadia Meiher, Svitlana Tarabarova. He has also starred in full-length movie “Infoholic” as cameo.

Keyboard instrument Hanna Berezniak

She is a winner of multiple music competitions and plays piano professionally from the age of six. Hanna has finished national musical academy of Ukraine named after Tchaikovsky. Before joining the band, she worked with a children chorus "Lastivka" (from Kyiv).

Bass guitar Andrey Romanenko

He has vast experience (Operetta Theatre of Kyiv, where he played bass guitar and contrabass; the National presidential orchestra; member of the bands Crescendo and Sharavara; Jazz orchestra). He was member of the music band that supported the Ukrainian Olympic team in Beijing and Vancouver. He performed on the stages of Ukraine, Russia, France, Netherlands, Spain, Austria, Germany, China, Sweden and Canada.
